History

A Journey Through Flavour

Our Humble Beginnings

The story of the 7 Stars Restaurant begins in the heart of the bustling sunshine city of Harare.

It was a chilly autumn evening when our founders, the seven members of the Stars family, decided to turn their passion for food into something extraordinary.

Armed with their grandmother's treasured recipes and an unwavering dream, they opened the doors of our restaurant in the year 2014.

The Turning Point

In 2019, a renowned food critic stumbled upon our unassuming corner.

His review catapulted us into the family dining spotlight.

Suddenly, reservations poured in, and our small restaurant expanded to accommodate the growing crowd.

Yet, amidst the accolades, we remained true to our roots, a place where friends became family over shared plates of linguine and laughter.

From Family Kitchen To Tasty Heaven

The early days were filled with laughter, spice-stained aprons, and the intoxicating aroma of simmering sauces.

The Stars' rustic local and exotic dishes quickly became the talk of the town.

Locals flocked to our restaurant, drawn by the promise of homemade tasty dishes and warm, heartfelt hospitality.

A Taste of Tradition

Our menu is a love letter to the Stars' Family.

Each dish carries the legacy of generations-hand-made, slow-cooked, crispy and tasty dishes.

The wooden tables, adorned with flickering candles, invite diners to savor every bite and create lasting memories.

Beyond the Plate

The 7Stars isn't just about food, it's about connection.

We've hosted first dates, anniversaries, and tearful farewells.

Our walls echo with whispered secrets and joyous celebrations.

Our staff, each with their own story, welcomes guests with open arms, making them feel like cherished guests at a homecoming feast.

Today and Tomorrow

As we celebrate our tenth anniversary, we honor the Stars' legacy.

Our commitment to quality, community, and authenticity remains unwavering.

Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, we invite you to join us at 7 Stars Family Restaurant.

Let's raise a glass to good food, great company, and the magic that happens when flavors intertwine.
